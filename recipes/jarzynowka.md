Polish vegetable salad (Jarzynówka)

- 1kg of carrots
- 2 parsley roots or 1/4th of a parsnip
- 4 eggs
- 3-4 apples
- 1 can of peas
- 1 glass of gherkins
- 1/4th of a leek
- Mayonnaise
- Apple cider vinegar
- Mustard
- Salt
- Pepper

1. Peel the carrots and parsley roots/parsnip
2. Cook the carrots, the parsley roots/parsnip and the eggs
3. Cut carrots, parsley roots/parsnip, eggs and apples into small cubes. There are grid shaped tools which make this easier.
4. Cut the gherkins and the leek into small pieces
5. Mix everything (don't forget the peas) with mayonnaise. Use a large enough container!
6. Mix in mustard, salt and pepper to taste.
7. Adjust the level of acid with gherkin water and apple cider vinegar to taste.
8. Store in the fridge overnight.

If cooled properly, this should not spoil up to a week. But it is best the next day!
